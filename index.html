Brisbane Internet Safety Meetup

Spreading privacy and security in Brisbane

Remember the internet of the 90s and early 2000s? Back then, you could say what you want without fear of "going on a list" or being blackmailed in the future. The only rule was never to reveal your real name or identity. As technical people, we have a duty to bring that internet back and make it the mainstream again. We are a bunch of techies and security experts who want to share our knowledge and make effective online security more accessable in Brisbane.

Our Last Event

Our first event was hosted at the ThoughtWorks office with a special guest, former senator for Queensland and active Greens member, Andrew Bartlett. The night was themed private browsing and devoted to protecting yourself from corporate trackers (facebook, twitter, ad networks) and from mandatory data retention recently introduced in Australia. We got a great mix of people who had no problem in installing the tools and had many interesting conversations throughout the night.
